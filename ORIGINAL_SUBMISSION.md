## Idea
Label Padegha Sabh

AI-Powered Consumer Decision Intelligence Platform

Proposal

Problem Statement

Every day, millions of consumers purchase packaged foods, medicines, and cosmetic products believing that the information printed on the label is enough to make an informed decision. However, product labels are often filled with complex scientific terminology, technical ingredient names, small print, and marketing claims that are difficult for the average person to understand.

As a result, consumers unknowingly expose themselves to ingredients that may conflict with their allergies, dietary preferences, age, medical conditions, or personal health goals. Even when products comply with regulations, consumers rarely understand what they are actually buying.

The problem is not the lack of information—it is the lack of understandable information.

This information gap contributes to poor purchasing decisions, misuse of products, preventable health risks, and reduced consumer confidence.

---

Social Impact

Label Padegha Sabh empowers consumers with transparent, evidence-based information instead of confusing technical jargon or misleading marketing claims.

The platform helps people:

- Understand product ingredients in simple language.
- Identify ingredients that deserve attention based on their personal profile.
- Access verified regulatory information from trusted authorities.
- Stay informed about official product recalls and safety alerts.
- Make informed purchasing decisions rather than relying solely on advertisements or packaging.

The platform promotes health awareness without replacing doctors, nutritionists, or regulatory agencies.

Instead of telling consumers what to buy, it helps them understand what they are buying.

---

Proposed Solution

Label Padegha Sabh is an AI-powered Consumer Decision Intelligence Platform.

Using a smartphone camera or barcode scanner, users can instantly analyze packaged products.

The platform combines Artificial Intelligence, barcode technology, regulatory databases, and ingredient intelligence to transform complex labels into simple, understandable insights.

Every analysis is personalized according to the user's profile, including:

- Age
- Allergies
- Dietary preferences
- Health conditions
- Lifestyle choices

---

Six-Factor Decision Intelligence Engine

1. Attention Level

Highlights ingredients that may require additional attention without declaring products as "safe" or "unsafe."

---

2. Ingredient Purpose Analysis

Explains:

- Why each ingredient is included
- Its functional purpose
- Consumer-friendly explanation

---

3. Global Regulatory Comparison

Shows how ingredients are regulated across different countries using trusted regulatory authorities such as:

- FSSAI
- FDA
- EFSA
- Health Canada
- FSANZ

---

4. Chemical Context Explanation

Converts difficult scientific names into simple, everyday language.

Example:

- Ascorbic Acid → Vitamin C
- Sodium Bicarbonate → Baking Soda

---

5. Personalized Warnings

Generates personalized alerts based on:

- Allergies
- Pregnancy
- Age
- Diabetes
- Hypertension
- Dietary restrictions
- Other user preferences

---

6. Verified News and Recall Information

Provides official product recalls and regulatory updates from trusted sources to keep consumers informed about important safety notices.

---

Key Features

- Barcode Scanner
- Product Label OCR
- AI-powered Ingredient Analysis
- Personalized Consumer Insights
- Regulatory Intelligence

- Product Comparison

---

Innovation

Unlike traditional product-rating applications that assign arbitrary scores or recommend products without explanation, Label Padegha Sabh focuses on transparency.

It does not certify, approve, or reject products.

Instead, it explains:

- What each ingredient is.
- Why it is present.
- How it is regulated.
- What it means for the individual consumer.

This creates a trustworthy decision-support system rather than an opinion-based rating platform.

Future Scope

The platform is designed to expand beyond packaged foods into:

- Medicines
- Cosmetics
- Nutritional supplements
- Household chemicals
- Baby care products
- Personal care products

Future enhancements include multilingual support, voice-based accessibility, wearable integration, predictive health insights, and global regulatory coverage.

---

Conclusion

Label Padegha Sabh is more than a barcode scanner—it is a Consumer Decision Intelligence Platform that bridges the gap between complex product information and everyday consumer understanding.

By transforming technical labels into clear, personalized, and evidence-based insights, the platform empowers individuals to make informed decisions that protect their health, improve awareness, and promote responsible consumption.

Its vision is simple:

"Don't just read the label. Understand it before you trust it."
